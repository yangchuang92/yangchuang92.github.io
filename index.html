About Me

Researcher of Swarm Navigation in Robotics and Neuroscience. From Neuroscience to Embedded Artificial Intelligence.

Mr. Chuang Yang (杨闯) is a PhD Student of Guidance, Navigation and Control (GNC) at Navigation Research Center (NRC) (导航研究中心), Nanjing University of Aeronautics and Astronautics (NUAA) (南京航空航天大学), China. Currently, he is working on his PhD thesis topic Brain-inspired swarm navigation for UAVs towards Neuromorphic Swarm . His current advisors are Prof.Jianye Liu (刘建业教授) and Prof.Zhi Xiong (熊智研究员). Before coming to NUAA, he graduated from Henan Polytechnic University (HPU), China, and received the master’s degree in GNC with final thesis Research on inertial/magnetic integrated navigation technique for measurement while drilling. His graduate advisor is Dr.Jinxian Yang.

He is a member of Royal Institute of Navigation(RIN), Chinese Society of Aeronautics and Astronautics(CSAA), and Chinese Association for Artificial Intelligence(CAAI). He is also a Journal Reviewer of Chinese Journal of Aeronautics (CJA).
