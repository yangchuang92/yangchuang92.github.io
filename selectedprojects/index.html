My Ongoing Research Projects

Project: NeuromorphicSWARM: Brain-inspired swarm navigation for UAVs (2019.12-2022.12)

This research project will fuse neural basis of bat’s 3D navigation & swarm navigation, collective behavior of animal groups and the decentralized cooperative navigation by employing neuroscience-oriented artificial intelligence method (e.g. CANN, ML, SNN) to develop a new class of brain-inspired swarm navigation architecture—towards Neuromorphic Swarm—of UAV Swarm.

More info on neuromorphicswarm.com

My Completed Research Projects

Project: Research on on-line monitoring system of transmission line galloping based on inertial technology (2016.09-2017.09)

In this research project, a principle prototype of on-line monitoring system of transmission line galloping was designed to measure and estimate the degree of the vibration amplitudes of the transmission line.

More info on On-line monitoring system of transmission line’s galloping

Project: Key technologies of MEMS inertial navigation for measurement while drilling (2015.01-2017.12)

In this research project, a principle prototype of magnetic/inertial-based Measurement While Drilling (MWD) surveying system was designed to measure and estimate the orientation (i.e. inclination, tool face angle and azimuth) of bottom hole assembly (BHA) while drilling.

More info on Orientation estimation system for MWD
