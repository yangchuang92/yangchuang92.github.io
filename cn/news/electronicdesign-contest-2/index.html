On-line monitoring system of transmission line’s galloping

In order to effectively reduce and control vibration of transmission lines,a transmission line vibration analysis method was proposed with a time series piecewise model based on parameters inertial measurement. Firstly,a MIMU ( micro inertial measurement unit) measurement node was designed with the MIMU fixed on a certain length elastic rod fastened on a transmission line for amplifying the MIMU signal physically and decreasing magnetic interference,and the collected MIMU signals were processed with the AR-Kalman filtering algorithm. Then the processed MIMU data were divided into different data segments with the static MIMU data as the benchmark segment. The transmission line vibration amplitudes were predicted by analyzing the variability between the subsequent segments and the benchmark one,and its vibration frequency was predicted by calculating the similarity of the adjacent two segments.

Overview

The system consists of three parts: Motion Surveying Unit, Software for PC and Software for Android.

  • Hardware: Arduino, Accelerometer, Gyroscope, Magnetometer
  • Software: LabVIEW for PC, LabVIEW for Android
