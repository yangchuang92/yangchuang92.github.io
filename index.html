About Me

Researcher of Brain-inspired Swarm Navigation in Neuroscience and Robotics. From software to hardware, theoretical research to engineering application.

Mr. Chuang Yang (杨闯) is now a PhD Student of Guidance, Navigation and Control (GNC) at Navigation Research Center (NRC), Nanjing University of Aeronautics and Astronautics (NUAA), China. Currently, he is working on his PhD thesis topic Brain-inspired swarm navigation for UAVs.
