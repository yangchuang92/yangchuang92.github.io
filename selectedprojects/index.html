NeuromorphicSWARM: Brain-inspired swarm navigation for UAVs (2019.12-2022.12)

This research project will fuse neural basis of bat’s 3D navigation and swarm navigation and the conventional integrated navigation by employing neuroscience-oriented artificial intelligence method to develop a new class of brain-inspired swarm navigation architecture—towards Neuromorphic Swarm—of UAV Swarm.
